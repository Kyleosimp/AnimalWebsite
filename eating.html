GOAT DIET

Goats will eat hay, grasses, weeds, grain, and sometimes even tree bark!

  • Goats are herbivores, which means they eat only vegetation.
  • The average adult goat requires about 4 pounds of hay per day to thrive.
  • Best way of feeding your goat is twice a day, meaning about 2 pounds of hay in the morning and the other 2 pounds in the evening.

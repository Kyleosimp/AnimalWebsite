GOAT BEHAVIOR

Goats can be quite aggressive and inquisitive so be careful!

  • Goats will point their ears forward if they're happy.
  • Goats often breath or pant rapidly when stressed.
  • Goats communicate through bleating with their fellow goats.

If you would like to learn more about their specific requirements, checkout this page!

Capra Aegagrus Hircus

Capra Hircus, otherwise known as the goat or the domestic goat is a domesticated species of goat-antelope typically kept as livestock. It was domesticated from the wild goat, Capra Aegagrus of Southwest Asia and Eastern European descent.

There are many different goat subspecies from Mountain Goats, to Pygmy Goats, Savanna goats and many more.
